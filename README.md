## 🚀 About Me

**ML Engineer** with 3+ years building production AI systems across healthcare, retail, and research domains. Currently **AI Engineer at Falabella**, developing computer vision and ML solutions for large-scale logistics operations.

I specialize in taking AI from concept to production—architecting end-to-end ML infrastructure, optimizing pipelines, and delivering systems that drive measurable business impact. My background combines a rigorous **Computer Science & Mathematics foundation** (dual degree, 4.7/5.0) with hands-on expertise in cloud deployment and MLOps.

**Highlights:**
- 🏭 Building CV and anomaly detection systems at **Falabella** on GCP infrastructure
- 🏥 Built HIPAA-compliant clinical AI systems at **GenomAI** with RAG and fine-tuned LLMs
- 🎓 **Research Associate at Harvard University** (3 years, ML pipelines & organizational modeling)
- 🏆 **LATAM-wide recognition** at Ipsos for TextInsight (NLP automation library)
